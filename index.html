                  A college symposium is a special type of academic gathering that typically involves multiple presentations or discussions on a particular subject, issue or theme, usually held on the campus of a college or university. These events bring together students, professors, and experts from different disciplines to exchange ideas, share knowledge, and showcase research findings.
                  College symposia can take many forms, ranging from formal lectures and presentations to informal panel discussions and roundtable conversations. The goal is to foster a lively and engaging exchange of ideas, often resulting in new insights and perspectives on the topic at hand. Students and faculty members alike can benefit from these events, as they offer an opportunity to learn from leading experts in their field, network with colleagues, and expand their understanding of complex topics. Overall, college symposia are an important part of academic life, providing a platform for intellectual engagement and discovery.

Bethlahem Institute of Engineering

                  Bethlahem Institute of Engineering (BIoE) is affiliated to Anna University Chennai and approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. This Institution was established in the year 2008 with a vision to provide focused technical education to empower budding technocrats with technical and entrepreneurial skills so as to become globally competent. The Institution intends to provide academic and career opportunities to all sectors of the society especially the socially and economically deprived younger generations of the societyThe Institution is managed by the Nesamony Memorial Trust and Shri. Gerald Selvaraja, a great visionary and renowned entrepreneur in the US is the Founder cum Chairman. The Trust started its academic journey with Bethlahem Matriculation School in the year 2002 and gradually added five more Institutions viz. Bethlahem College of Education (2006),Bethlahem Institute of Engineering (2008), Bethlahem Hillside International School (2017),Bethlehem New Central School (2018), Bethlahem College of Arts and Science (2019).

                  The Institution is well known for its Technical Excellence, modern facilities and Eco-friendly campus which is conducive for the all-round development of learners. The campus provides an ideal ambience for young minds to explore the unexplored, stimulate creativity and independent thinking. Its adherence to high academic standards, strict internal evaluation, state-of-the-art infrastructure, technically qualified faculty and exciting opportunities offered, make it a destination for Engineering aspirants..

CSE & IT Department

                  The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Bethlahem Institute of Engineering was started in the year 2008. The department has a unique team of teaching fraternity and facilities that offer students a platform to strengthen their skills. The department offers an impressive atmosphere for academic and research so as to make them globally competent in the current technical scenario.

                  The department of Information Technology aims at moulding the young technocrats into Information Technology professionals. The IT sector is growing rapidly so as the job opportunities during this Digital era. The students are offered with deep theoretical and practical learning, for the perfect blend of technical and managerial skills.
